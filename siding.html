Services

Siding we install, repair and replace

Transform Your Home with Vinyl Siding

Vinyl siding is a popular and affordable option for homeowners looking to enhance their property's appearance and durability. Our team has extensive experience installing vinyl siding, using only high-quality materials and modern installation techniques to ensure a perfect fit and long-lasting results.

Upgrade to Durable and Stylish Hardie Siding

James Hardie fiber cement siding is a premium option for homeowners looking for exceptional durability and resistance to the elements. Our team is fully trained and experienced in installing Hardie siding, ensuring a precise fit and flawless finish for your property

Enhance Your Home's Natural Beauty with Cedar Siding

Cedar siding offers a natural and rustic look for your property, and our team has the expertise to ensure a proper installation. We use only the highest-quality cedar siding materials and modern installation techniques to ensure your cedar siding is not only beautiful but also long-lasting.

Discover the Benefits of Low-Maintenance Aluminum Siding

Aluminum siding is a durable and low-maintenance option for homeowners looking to enhance their property's exterior. Our team is highly skilled in installing aluminum siding, ensuring a perfect fit and a beautiful finish for your home or business.

Protect Your Home from Moisture Damage with Expert Soffit Installation

Soffit installation is an important aspect of any exterior renovation, as it provides ventilation for your property and prevents moisture buildup. Our team has extensive experience installing soffit, using high-quality materials and precise installation techniques to ensure optimal ventilation and a beautiful finish.

Ensure Optimal Drainage and Protect Your Property with Professional Gutter Installation

Proper gutter installation is essential for protecting your property from water damage and ensuring proper drainage. Our team is highly experienced in installing gutters of all types, including seamless gutters, to ensure maximum protection for your home or business.

Commonly Asked Questions

The length of time it takes to install siding depends on several factors, including the size of the property and the type of siding being installed. Our team at Rovas Construction will provide you with a detailed timeline for your specific project.

In some cases, it may be possible to install new siding over existing siding. However, our team at Rovas Construction recommends removing the existing siding to ensure the new siding is properly installed and provides maximum durability.

The durability of siding depends on several factors, including the material, quality, and installation. At Rovas Construction, we offer a variety of durable siding options, including steel and aluminum siding, that are designed to withstand harsh weather conditions and maintain their appearance for years to come.

The lifespan of siding varies depending on the material and quality of the installation. Generally, siding should be replaced every 15 to 25 years. However, if you notice signs of damage or wear, such as cracking or warping, it may be necessary to replace it sooner.
